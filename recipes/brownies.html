Brownies

Description

These brownies with chocolate chips are absolutely the best I've ever tasted. The rich, fudgy texture combined with the sweetness of the chocolate chips creates an indulgent treat that's hard to resist. Every bite is a perfect blend of gooey chocolate goodness and delightful crunch from the chips. Whether enjoyed warm out of the oven or cooled to perfection, these brownies stand out as a favorite dessert, delivering an unforgettable taste experience every time.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 1/2 cups of semisweet chocolate chips
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 2/3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Grease an 8-inch square pan.
  3. Combine sugar, butter, and water in a medium saucepan; cook and stir over medium heat until boiling. Remove from heat and stir in chocolate chips until melted and smooth; mix in eggs and vanilla. Combine flour, salt, and baking soda; stir into the chocolate mixture. Spread brownie batter evenly into the prepared pan.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until top is dry and edges have started to pull away from the sides of the pan, about 20 to 30 minutes.
  5. Let cool completely before cutting into squares.
